This is Ben. He is Donkey Kong

Ben's Background

Ben was born at a very young age. He liked to run and play stupid games like Warzone, one of the most terrible games in the history of games. He is mean.

Ben's Midlife

When Ben was in his 10s-20s, Ben was easily tricked by his friends Liam and Khalijiah. One day, he got so sick of it that he decided to bamboozle them in all of their Warzone games. This made the two so mad that they took his laptop and deleted his account. Ben started to fume and then ran all across the world and back in 3 seconds. Both of the bullies were so shocked that they decided to give Ben all of their money.

Ben's Billion Years

When Ben got all of their money, he decided to get 400 new pairs of running shoes for distance cross country to beat all of the B O O M E R S. This, unfortunately, didn't work and then Ben returned his shoes. Ben decided to invest in a hedge fund-like Tom Styer-and about a year later, he got 2 billion dollars. He then decided to become a professional Warzone gamer.

Ben's Later Years

Ben was getting tired of playing Warzone professionally and making millions of dollars. He wanted to retire and so he did. He gained about 400 million dollars in profit for playing for 25 years. He decided that his life was worthwhile to him and so he went on vacation for a few years in the Bahamas.

Ben's Last Moments

Ben was 201 years old at this point. He had lived his life to the fullest and he had been complacent with his achievements. Sitting on his hospital bed was Cooper, his 4000 year old dog who had grown up to become a professional basketball player for the Boston Celtics, the best and worst team known to man (in terms of basketball).
